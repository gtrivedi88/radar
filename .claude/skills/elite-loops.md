# Elite Loops

Autonomous monitoring that **works like your brain** - obsessive about quality, intolerant of obvious takes, focused on actionable specifics.

## Your Loop Psychology

**You want loops that**:
- Catch things **before they become obvious**
- Filter out generic AI hype automatically  
- Alert with **specific actions**, not vague "something's happening"
- Match your confrontational, contrarian style
- Respect your time (no false positives)

**You don't want**:
- Alerts about trending AI news everyone's covering
- Generic "this is interesting" notifications
- Loops that produce consultant-speak analysis
- Monitoring that creates work instead of reducing it

## Elite Loop Templates

### 1. Contrarian Signal Hunter
```bash
/loop 2d "Hunt for contrarian signals in technical discussions. Look for: high-engagement posts opposing consensus, infrastructure changes contradicting hype, performance data disproving popular beliefs. ALERT ONLY if: >500 engagement + contradicts mainstream + has specific evidence + matches Gaurav's beat. Format alert as specific post headline + 'Ship by [date]'"
```

### 2. Your-Beat Opportunity Detector  
```bash
/loop 1w "Monitor intersection of: Claude/AI tools + technical writing + workflow optimization. TRIGGER when: community pain point (>50 complaints) + no existing solution + Gaurav could solve uniquely. Output: Specific course/post/tool idea + effort estimate + timeline + validation approach."
```

### 3. Positioning Window Alerts
```bash
/loop 3d "Track early signals in: developer tools, documentation automation, AI-powered workflows. ALERT when: technology reaches 'early adopter' stage (not R&D, not mainstream) + competitors haven't covered angle + audience is ready. Format: 'WRITE NOW: [specific headline] - [why this week, not next month]'"
```

### 4. Competition Gap Monitor
```bash
/loop 1w "Monitor what top technical writing influencers + AI education creators are NOT covering. Look for: pain points they ignore, tools they haven't tried, angles they avoid. ALERT when: significant gap + growing community need + fits Gaurav's expertise. Output: Specific content idea + competitive differentiation."
```

## Loop Quality Filters

**Before any alert fires, verify**:
✅ **Contrarian**: Opposes or ignores mainstream narrative
✅ **Evidenced**: Has data/metrics, not just opinions  
✅ **Actionable**: Can produce specific content in <10 hours
✅ **Timed**: Why now, not in 6 months
✅ **Differentiated**: Unique angle vs existing coverage
✅ **Beat-matched**: Hits AI tools + technical writing + workflows

**Auto-suppress alerts for**:
❌ Generic "AI trends" content
❌ Obvious takes everyone's covering
❌ Technology still in pure R&D phase
❌ Consumer AI applications (vs. professional tools)
❌ Abstract strategy without implementation details

## Alert Format (Your Style)

**NOT THIS**:
> "Emerging trend detected in HTML-first development. Consider strategic analysis of market positioning opportunities."

**THIS**:
> "🎯 WRITE NOW: 'HTML-First Beats React: The Performance Data' 
> Evidence: 1065 HN points, 480 comments, developers realizing JS bloat costs users
> Angle: While everyone debates frameworks, simple HTML wins  
> Timeline: Ship by Friday while thread is hot
> Competition: Zero quality takes on this angle yet
> Effort: 4 hours (data exists, just need your voice)"

## Loop Success Metrics

**Elite loops deliver**:
- 1-2 high-quality alerts per week (not noise)
- Specific actions, not research tasks
- Opportunities 2-4 weeks before they become obvious
- Content ideas that differentiate from competition
- Contrarian angles backed by evidence

**Failure signals**:
- Generic trend alerts everyone's seeing
- Vague "this might be interesting" notifications  
- Alerts about things already covered everywhere
- Recommendations requiring extensive research

## Advanced: Recursive Research Loops

When signals strengthen, loops auto-research deeper:

```bash
/loop 5d "IF HTML-first signal strengthens (>2000 total engagement OR competitor starts covering), THEN auto-research: performance benchmarks, developer adoption data, framework comparison metrics. Output: Complete post outline + evidence package + publishing strategy."
```

This creates **autonomous intelligence** that works **exactly how your brain works** - obsessive about quality, allergic to obvious takes, focused on actionable specifics with contrarian angles.
