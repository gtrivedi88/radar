# Specific Actions

Transform signals into **exact things to do** - not vague strategic recommendations.

## Your Action Psychology

**You want**:
- Specific deadlines: "Write by Friday"
- Exact formats: "2000-word post" not "content piece"  
- Clear positioning: "Why X beats Y" not "Exploring X vs Y"
- Effort estimates: "3 hours" not "significant investment"

**You don't want**:
- "Consider exploring..."
- "This might be worth..."
- "Potential opportunity for..."
- Generic strategic frameworks

## Action Templates

### Content Actions
```markdown
**POST**: [Exact headline]
**ANGLE**: [Your specific contrarian take]  
**LENGTH**: [Word count target]
**EFFORT**: [Writing time estimate]
**DEADLINE**: [Ship by when]
**HOOK**: [Opening that grabs attention]
**EVIDENCE**: [Data/examples to include]
**CTA**: [How readers should respond]
```

**Example**:
```markdown
**POST**: "HTML-First Beats React: Performance Data Nobody's Talking About"
**ANGLE**: While devs debate frameworks, HTML-first doubled someone's users overnight
**LENGTH**: 1500 words
**EFFORT**: 4 hours (2 hours research, 2 hours writing)
**DEADLINE**: Friday 2026-06-14
**HOOK**: "1065 HN upvotes can't be wrong - HTML is eating JavaScript's lunch"
**EVIDENCE**: HN case study + performance benchmarks + developer adoption data
**CTA**: "Test HTML-first on your next project - here's the starter template"
```

### Course Actions

```markdown
**COURSE**: [Exact title and positioning]
**MODULES**: [Specific module breakdown]
**TIMELINE**: [Build schedule with milestones]
**PRICE**: [Specific pricing strategy] 
**VALIDATION**: [How to test demand first]
**COMPETITION**: [Differentiation from existing courses]

```

### Speaking Actions
```markdown
**TALK**: [Exact title]
**CFP**: [Specific conference + deadline]
**ABSTRACT**: [Submission strategy]
**DEMO**: [What you'll show live]
**PREP TIME**: [Practice schedule]

```

## Effort Calibration

**Your working style** (from observing your pace):
- **High-impact posts**: 4-6 hours including research
- **Course modules**: 2-3 weeks per module for quality
- **Talk prep**: 1 week for new content, 2 days for refined content
- **Research deep-dives**: 3-4 hours for thorough analysis

## Priority Scoring

**Ship This Week** (Score 9-10):
- Contrarian angle + strong evidence + audience ready + low competition
- Example: HTML-first post while HN thread is hot

**Build Next Month** (Score 7-8):  
- Good opportunity + moderate competition + needs some research
- Example: Claude performance optimization guide

**Watch for Q4** (Score 5-6):
- Emerging signal + unclear timing + need more validation
- Example: AI-first documentation tooling

**Ignore** (Score 1-4):
- Obvious take + high competition + late to party
- Example: "AI will transform writing" content

## Action Quality Gates

**Before recommending any action, verify**:
✅ **Specific**: Exact deliverable, not vague direction
✅ **Timed**: Clear deadline, not "eventually"  
✅ **Effortless**: Realistic time estimate based on your pace
✅ **Differentiated**: Unique angle, not rehashing others
✅ **Audience-matched**: Hits tech writers OR devs curious about AI OR hardcore tech
✅ **Evidence-backed**: Data supports the contrarian position

**Auto-reject if**:
❌ Requires explaining basic AI concepts
❌ Sounds like existing content in your niche  
❌ Would take >20 hours for unclear ROI
❌ Appeals to generic "business" audiences
❌ Lacks concrete implementation details

This ensures every action recommendation is **immediately actionable** and **psychologically aligned** with how you actually work.
